What Are We About?

McGivney Coding Club is a beginner-friendly club to learn programming. Students will gain experiencial knowledge through lessons/workshops, coding projects, and leetcode challenges. We here at MCC teach the fundamental concepts of software development to prepare you for multiple avenues of programming (competitive programming, app development, web development and more). McGivney Coding Club was created by Father Michael MGivney in Markham, Ontario
